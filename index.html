zG Era is the youth-led movement to preserve Zubeen Da’s legacy, protect Assam’s soul, and build the Golden Assam he dreamed of.

The Vision

Unity — Socialist — Ideologist

Preserving Zubeen Da’s legacy both physically and digitally. Turning his dream of a united, prosperous Assam into reality.

Preserve Zubeen Khetra

Protect and build the physical legacy — an open stage, statue, and cultural centre that will stand for generations.

Carry the Legacy

Keep Zubeen Da’s voice, music, and revolutionary ideas alive for every future generation of Assam.

Platform for Unity

A digital + offline space where Assamese youth collaborate, create, and take action together.

Golden Assam

Direct action for nature conservation, cultural revival, and building the prosperous Assam Zubeen Da envisioned.

The Roadmap

Our clear, step-by-step journey to make the dream reality.

Phase 01 — Now

Digital Community Launch

Building the core family through this waitlist. First 500 members become “Early Supporters” with direct access to planning the first nature drive.

Phase 02 — Q2 2026

Ground Action Begins

Cultural meets, river clean-ups, and local events across Assam to turn vision into visible change.

Phase 03 — Q3 2026

Full zG Era Platform

Dedicated app/website for creators, volunteers, donations, and real-time movement coordination.

Phase 04 — 2027 Onwards

Zubeen Khetra Reality

Permanent cultural centre, open stage, and statue in the heart of Assam — the physical home of the legend.
